Phaser 3 Game

Scene 1

Press W to move Up, S to go Down,
A to go Left, D to go Right,
Press Spacebar to jump up.
Press E to see Fire
Press 2, 3, 4, or 5 to jump to a scene.

Scene 2

Bouncing Sentence
Press 3 to go to scene 3.

Scene 3

Press W to move Up, S to go Down,
A to go Left, D to go Right,
Press L to Loop the music or P to Pause the Music.
Press 4 to go to scene 4.

Scene 4

Message From the Captain
Press 5 to go to scene 5.

Scene 5

The End
Press E to see fire
Press 1 to return to scene 1.

Please be aware game currently runs in Edge only
